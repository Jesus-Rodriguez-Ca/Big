Social Media Optimization(SMO)

What is Social Media Optimization?

Social Media Optimization (SMO) is a technique that is used to enhance the visibility of content on social media and online communities, such as Instagram or blogs. The way that SMO works is through social networking, just as we do when we tell our friends what the best coffee in the neighborhood is, by word of mouth, but on the Web. SMO is a part of Social Media Marketing (SMM). There are many tactics, that can be used to optimize content online.

Some of those tactics are direct messaging, augmented reality, and live streaming. Direct message (DM ) is a method of direct communication with users. DM is a powerful tool that allows the server to set up a personalized connection. DM helps to establish better relationships with users than undirect methods. Although, it is important to know that the overuse of this tactic can end up with the opposite results.

As well as that, augmented reality (AR) is one of the new technologies that are and will be an important tool to increase the fluidity of servers content through the network. AR gives incredible interactivity to users. One example of this technology and its use is the game Pokemon Go. The game allows the users to move in the real world and play through the camera and the screen.

Live streaming is one important tactic that its use has been increasing. According to Mike Templeman from Foxtail, people spend 3 times longer watching live streamings than prerecorded videos. Also, Lindsay Kolowich says in her blog blog.hubspot that 73% of consumers worldwide prefer seeing videos on social media. This information gives a good sight that live streaming is an effective tactic to give more presence to content.

As has been noted, Social Media Optimization is a technique to increase the presence of servers in social media. Direct messaging, augmented reality, and live streaming are three important tools that can be used to drive more traffic to the desired content. SMO is a good practice for those who want their content to have more presence on the web.
